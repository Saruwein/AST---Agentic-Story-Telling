## Reader analysis

Refer to the story in natural language only. Describe what the text
says, not what metadata claims.

**Summarise** — concise plot summary of the requested scope.

**Depict** — describe a character as they appear in the manuscript.

**Assess** — does a scene, passage, or story point work? What feeling
does it create?

**Vibe** — what atmosphere or emotional tenor is the story projecting?
